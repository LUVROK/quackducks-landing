WELCOME
Quack Ducks is a secret society of 6666 ducks of different backgrounds. There are 6 factions: Druglords, Degens, Mob, Artists, Engineers and Scientists. Every faction has its own perks and benefits. Choose your faction and join us on this journey.

factions

DRUGLORDS

They made their fortune by supplying drugs (fun pills as they call it). Crack, weed, acids, you name it. If you've ever tried any of those, that were the fruits of their labour. Always high, drunk and hallucinating because they have to try their stuff before distributing.

DEGENS

If they were on a spelling bee contest spelling "research", they would probably spell it as "ape in". Some of them lose everything, while others make millions. There's no explanation to this. I guess you can call them "gamblers", but they prefer "investors".

MOB

Guns and kidnapping, assassinations and soy sauce - that's what they love the most. If you ever need something done quietly and quickly, don't hit them up. They will probably explode something in the process.

ADVENTURERS

Geography masters, yeti experts, grizzly bear tamers. Message from them: 5.532383, -87.084435, "I live on an Island and I'm really high I love when it rains and I love when it shines I stand through the storms but I do fall sometimes You breath it, I clean it - do you know what am I?"

ENGINEERS

Say something bad about them, and they will turn you into a frog using an old microwave, 5 feet of cable and 2 packs of gum. Their inventions suck, but i'd advise you to be quiet.

SCIENTISTS

Least communicative out of the five other factions. Always studying the Space, taking measurements of the climate, conducting experiments with nitro-glycerine. It might be concerning, but you shouldn't worry, I promise.
VISION
Let's be honest, everyone is tired of false promises. We don't want to do that, but we can't launch the project just by telling everyone to buy some NFT's. Instead, we will walk you through our vision of the project, what we're capable of and what we intend to do.
Community
Speaking of, we want everyone to find a place where they can feel at home. Meaningful conversations, connections and friendship instead of "wen this" questions. Managing a community of thousands of people post-mint is no easy task, that's why we will be "splitting" the community in 6 parts, according to the factions. If you're a degen, you can discuss the next project to ape in with other degens, if you're a developer, you can always ask for an advice from someone more experienced than you. Want to participate in an IRL treasure hunt? Become an adventurer. Want to sell commissioned artwork/figurines/apparel? Become an artist. In short: find friends and build together. Ducks Together Strong!
Apparel
Every duck needs something to wear. We had experience with running an apparel business, so it's not a big deal. Our apparel has to be something that any person would wear without feeling cringey. We've come up with a unique way to customize apparel, it's easy and elegant. Apparel plays an important part of our project, as it will be the main source of income. Limited edition, highest quality. Collab drops, 1/1 collectibles and more.
Brand
We're not just a collection, we're a hub of the most talented people. We're a movement. Just imagine graffiti murals of your favourite ducks in your city, comic books on the shelves of your local shop, people wearing hoodies, jeans & custom sneakers with our branding on it. Watch Quack Ducks take over the world.
An actual roadmap
We want YOU to take part in developing our roadmap. Consider everything we wrote prior to this a foundation, a "skeleton" for our roadmap. Got an idea that other projects can't bring to life? We're always open for suggestions!
